# i vs ɪ


i 发音单词通常包含  ee (see, cheese),  ea (please, beat),  ie/ei (believe).


ɪ 发音单词通常包含  i (if, different, this, thing),  y  in middle (system, gym).

# S and Z


S 前是发声的浊辅音，S 发 Z 音。例如 lives, feels, cares。


S 前是不发声的清辅音，S 发 S 音。例如 books, laughs, mats。


通常情况下，S 前是元音， S 发 Z 音。

# Stops and Continuants


辅音的分类除了从发声和不发声区分外，还可以从停顿或连续音方面来分类。


停顿音通过堵住气流来发音，例如 job 不能连续发音 jobbbb.


连续音是可以连续发音的音并且发音需要延长，例如 yes 能连续发音 yesssss.

“T” followed by “you” is like “TU”, “D” followed by “you” is like “DU”.


Why can’t you do it?


Aren’t you hungry?

# ED


If last letter is T or D, “ed” is a separate syllable. waited, painted, needed, attended.


If last letter is voiceless, “ed” becomes a “T” sound. stopped, worked, passed, laughed, pushed, watched.


If last letter is voiced , “ed” becomes a “D” sound. robbed, loved, realised, used, called
